Let us assume that $X$ and $Y$ are independent random variables.
\[\text{var}[X+Y]=\mathbb{E}[(X+Y)^2]-\mathbb{E}[X+Y]^2\]
\[=\mathbb{E}[X^2+2XY+Y^2]-\left(\mathbb{E}[X]+\mathbb{E}[Y]\right)^2\]
